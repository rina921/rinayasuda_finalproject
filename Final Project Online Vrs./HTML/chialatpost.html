Food

CHALAIT | SOHO, NY

I love CHALAIT. In the heart of the West Village, they serve up their matcha drinks and espresso. They are known for their matcha lattes, which is what I indulged in….AND OMG it was so good!

They also serve light breakfast and lunch options which looked amazing and I can’t wait to try them. I was lucky to snag a seat at the bar and sat with my laptop and tea. While studying (researching articles on WWD, Harper’s Bazaar, and Vogue.com), I loved watching the baristas make the drinks! It was so intriguing and they made it look so easy. I highly recommend checking this place out if you like tea, cute cafés, and an “LA-like” atmosphere.
