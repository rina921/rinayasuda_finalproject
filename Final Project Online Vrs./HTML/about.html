About
Rina Yasuda

Rina Yasuda is a Japanese girl who loves to travel, eat, cook, explore, photograph, wander, learn, discover many wonderful things:) She's using this online space as a kind of electronic journal and time capsule; she wants to be able to look back over her adventures and remember them all, and she also wants to leave some sort of record of what life was like for a girl like her. Because, over time, things change. Lifestyles evolve and cities are re-built and all the rest of it; things are pretty good right here and now, and she wants that to be recorded somewhere, so that in years to come people can see what it was like “back in the day…”
She is currently in Boston finishing her last year of undergraduate program at Boston University She is often traveling between Asia and the States for work and travel.
